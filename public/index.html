Chibi is a mobile dating and networking service that helps you find new friends. Chibi is 100% anonymous, works on all phones and does not require an Internet connection. Just call or text to register and start chatting immediately.

How it works

  • Call or SMS

    You can contact Chibi using your mobile phone by either calling or sending a SMS to one of the numbers below. Chibi will instantly find you a new friend to chat with. You can continue to chat with your new friend or choose to find a new one at anytime.

  • Natural Language

    Chibi speaks your language. When calling or sending a SMS, Chibi will reply in your language. Futhermore, Chibi tries to figure out what you mean. For example, if you want to meet a girl, just write "I want to meet a girl" or "find me a girl!" etc. It even recognizes your language. For example if your texting from Cambodia, you can write "khnom jong rok met srey" or "rok awey srey moi" etc. No more complicated commands. Just say what you want.

  • Anonymous

    Chibi will never give out your number to anybody. When chatting, your number is blocked from your friend, who can only see your screen id.
